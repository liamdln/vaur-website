ABOUT US

Welcome to Virtual Aurigny!

Established in 2015, Over the years we have gained very useful information on what pilots need and try to give the best experiences Pilots can have here at Virtual Aurigny. If you would like to learn more about who we are, then please keep scrolling down. If you're already a Pilot with us then please do login, or if you're already hooked, please feel free to signup!

We believe we offer a completely unique Virtual Airline experience - with our PIREPs automatically processed, pilot reports for when things go wrong, public PIREPs and Profiles for boasting about those awesome arrivals (or sharing those mishaps...), leaderboards and ranks to add that competitive edge, and a whole host of exclusive resources to offer our pilots!
